A student society passionate about programming, designing, and building robots, as well as developing artificial intelligence applications.

MISSION

The mission of the Robotics Society is to foster a vibrant and inclusive community of students, researchers, and enthusiasts with a shared passion for robotics. Through a variety of activities, projects, and events, we aim to promote learning, creativity, and hands-on experience in robotics technology and its applications. Our goal is to empower students to become proficient in robotics, develop critical problem-solving skills, and contribute meaningfully to the advancement of robotics both within and beyond our university.

VISION


Our vision for the Robotics Society is to become a hub of innovation and excellence in robotics education and research within the university and beyond. We envision a dynamic community where students from diverse backgrounds and disciplines come together to explore the fascinating world of robotics, collaborate on innovative projects, and engage in cutting-edge research. By fostering a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and creativity . we aspire to inspire the next generation of robotics leaders and innovators who will shape the future of technology and society.

OBJECTIVES

Empowering Tomorrow's Workforce

To engage and enhance the skills of a diverse pool of passionate and proficient students, aligning their competencies with the demands of the labor market, and collaborating with them on projects.

Igniting Passion

To foster a vibrant student community with a keen interest in artificial intelligence, particularly addressing the existing gap in this domain.

Driving Innovation

To facilitate the organization of competitions at both university and national levels, positioning the University of Jordan as a prominent hub for robotics and artificial intelligence competitions and technical endeavors within Jordan.

Bridging Theory and Practice

To invite industry experts and offer comprehensive courses covering the technical facets of robotics, ranging from design principles to coding proficiency.

TEAM

TECHNICAL TEAM

The Technical team serves as our innovative core, where all project building takes place. Our skilled members specialize in programming, robotics, Arduino and organizing technical workshops for the team. Their unwavering commitment to excellence in the world of robotics shapes the future of our society.

LOGISTICS TEAM

The Logistics team serves as the backbone of Waves' events and workshops. Team members play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth execution of operations. Additionally, they handle fundraising, securing sponsors, and connecting with businesses, all of which are essential to our student society.

SOCIAL MEDIA TEAM

The Media team is the creative heartbeat of our society, capturing our events through their beautiful lense and sharing our achievements on our social media accounts. Team members are responsible for designing content across all aspects of the society, from engaging social media posts to captivating photography and videography.

DEVELOPMENT TEAM

The Development Team is responsible for creating and perfecting the Waves Society website. Within this team, members actively engage in both front and backend development, while also dedicating effort to its constant maintenance and enhancement.
